About Nextflow

Nextflow is a free and open source software distributed under the Apache 2.0 licence and developed by Seqera Labs. The software is used by scientists and engineers to write, deploy and share data-intensive, highly scalable, workflows on any infrastructure.

The project was started in the Notredame Lab at the Centre for Genomic Regulation (CRG).

Check it out the project repository on GitHub for details.

Project Funding

The project is supported and maintained by Seqera Labs.

The Chan Zuckerberg Initiative supports the project through two Essential Open Source Software for Science (EOSS) grants. Together with nf-core, Nextflow is part of of the second and fourth cycles.
